**1. Business Name:** Wholesome Bites  
**Type:** Restaurant  
**Rating:** ★★★★★  
**Review:** If you are looking for a place where you can enjoy a healthy yet utterly satisfying meal, Wholesome Bites is your destination. As a community nurse, I really appreciate the menu here, which focuses on fresh ingredients and balanced meals. The roasted vegetable quinoa bowl is my absolute favorite. The staff is friendly and attentive, and their dedication to providing nutritious options is apparent. Plus, they host occasional health talks that align perfectly with my passion for promoting wellness. Highly recommend!

**2. Business Name:** Green Wave Hair Salon  
**Type:** Hair Salon  
**Rating:** ★★★★☆  
**Review:** I recently decided to try Green Wave Hair Salon based on a friend’s recommendation and I am quite pleased. **3. Business Name:** The Lucky Wrench  
**Type:** Mechanic  
**Rating:** ★★★★☆  
**Review:** Finding a reliable mechanic isn't always easy, but The Lucky Wrench deserves your trust. I had an issue with my brakes, and they diagnosed it quickly and efficiently. The staff explained everything in layman's terms so I could make an informed decision. It's apparent they value transparency, which makes me feel secure in the services they provide. Very fair pricing, too. I will return for any future car problems.

**4. Business Name:** Time Traveller Books  
**Type:** Bookstore

I found a rare edition of my favorite novel, and I've been a regular ever since. It's a wonderful spot to lose a couple of hours on a rainy Dalston afternoon.

**5. Business Name:** La Vela  
**Type:** Italian Restaurant (on a trip to Brighton)  
**Rating:** ★★★★☆  
**Review:** On a weekend trip to Brighton, I had the pleasure of dining at La Vela. The coastal views are breathtaking, and the seafood specials are not to be missed. I ordered the linguine alle vongole, and it was delightful—fresh and seasoned just right. The staff made us feel like family, which was a nice touch. It's slightly on the pricier side, but well worth it for a special occasion or a treat during a seaside getaway.

**6. Business Name:** Dalston Health Hub  
**Type:** Wellness Center  
**Rating:** ★★★★★  
**Review:** As someone who often stresses the importance of holistic wellbeing, Dalston Health Hub has become my go-to sanctuary.
