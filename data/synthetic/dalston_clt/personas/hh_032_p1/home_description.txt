As a retiree, she owns the apartment, reflecting her years of prudent living and saving. The living room is the heart of the flat, where the aroma of traditional Turkish dishes often wafts from the small but well-organized kitchen. Her love for gardening is evident in the array of potted plants that thrive on her windowsills, bringing bursts of green into the space. Zeynep’s flat is tidy and welcoming, with an eclectic mix of vintage and modern furniture. There is a cozy reading nook by the window, where she enjoys sipping tea while reminiscing or diving into a good book. Her home is a warm and inviting sanctuary that mirrors her nurturing personality.
