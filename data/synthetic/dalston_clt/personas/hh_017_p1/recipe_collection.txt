**Linda Huang's Recipe Collection**

1. **Vegetable Stir-Fry**  
   Quick and easy, this dish features a mix of colorful vegetables like bell peppers, broccoli, and snap peas. Perfect for a nutritious meal in under 30 minutes.

2. **Miso Soup**  
   A comforting and simple Japanese soup made with miso paste, tofu, seaweed, and green onions. A go-to dish that pairs beautifully with any meal.

3. **Chickpea Salad**  
   A refreshing salad with canned chickpeas, diced cucumbers, tomatoes, red onion, and a lemon-tahini dressing. Healthy and ready in just 15 minutes.

4. **Lemon Garlic Roasted Chicken Thighs**  
   Easy to prepare with minimal active cooking time, this dish involves marinating chicken thighs in lemon juice and garlic before roasting. Perfect for a satisfying dinner.

5. **Congee (Rice Porridge)**  
   A warm and comforting breakfast option, this rice porridge can be topped with green onions, pickled vegetables, or a drizzle of soy sauce. Simple to make and filling.

6. **Vegetable Samosas**  
   These baked samosas are filled with spiced potatoes and peas. They take a bit longer to prepare but are a delightful treat for occasional gatherings.

7. **Spaghetti Aglio e Olio**  
   A classic Italian dish that's quick to whip up, made with spaghetti, garlic, olive oil, and a sprinkle of red pepper flakes. Perfect for a late-night craving.

8. **Green Tea Smoothie**  
   A refreshing drink made with brewed green tea, spinach, banana, and almond milk. Healthy and easy to blend up in the morning.

9. **Stuffed Bell Peppers**  
   These colorful bell peppers filled with quinoa, black beans, and spices can be prepped ahead of time and are perfect for a fulfilling meal during the week.

10. **Fruit Salad with Honey-Lime Dressing**  
    A quick and healthy dessert or snack option with a mix of seasonal fruits. The honey-lime dressing adds a touch of sweetness and brightness.
