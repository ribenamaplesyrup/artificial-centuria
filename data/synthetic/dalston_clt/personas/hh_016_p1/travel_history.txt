Over the past 2-3 years, Jonathan McCarthy has taken a handful of memorable trips that reflect his love for art, history, and culture. As a retiree on a budget-conscious travel style, he has chosen destinations that offer rich historical experiences and opportunities for cultural enrichment.

1. **Edinburgh, Scotland** - In the summer of 2021, Jonathan visited Edinburgh, drawn by its historical architecture and cultural festivals. He particularly enjoyed exploring Edinburgh Castle and the National Museum of Scotland. The trip was planned using a combination of travel apps and recommendations from friends.

2. **Florence, Italy** - Jonathan's love for art took him to Florence in the spring of 2022. Fascinated by the Renaissance, he spent his days visiting the Uffizi Gallery and the Florence Cathedral. He traveled here using a modest package deal that included guided tours, allowing him to fully immerse himself in the city's artistic heritage.

3. **Bath, England** - A short domestic trip in autumn 2022 brought Jonathan to Bath, where he indulged in the history of the Roman Baths and the Georgian architecture. This trip was a self-organized weekend getaway, booked directly through a travel app.

4. **Bruges, Belgium** - In early 2023, Jonathan embarked on a cultural exploration of Bruges, captivated by its medieval buildings and canals. He participated in a guided walking tour to delve deeper into the city's history. This trip was booked through an agent who offered a budget-friendly travel package.

Jonathan typically books his travels using travel apps for simplicity and occasionally engages with travel agents for special packages. 

**Bucket List Destinations:**

1. **Kyoto, Japan** - Jonathan dreams of visiting Kyoto to experience its traditional temples, gardens, and tea ceremonies.
2. **Vienna, Austria** - Vienna's classical music scene and historical museums are high on his list.
3. **Petra, Jordan** - The ancient city of Petra appeals to his interest in history and archaeology.

Though Jonathan travels selectively, his trips are carefully chosen to ensure they are culturally enriching and aligned with his interests.
