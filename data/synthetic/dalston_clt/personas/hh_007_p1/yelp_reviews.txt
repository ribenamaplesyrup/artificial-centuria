**Business Name:** The Green Leaf Bistro  
**Type:** Restaurant  
**Rating:** ★★★★☆  
**Review:** I've enjoyed quite a few meals at The Green Leaf Bistro. Their vegetarian options are delightful, and I've always appreciated the seasonal menu. The staff is friendly, and the atmosphere reminds me of cozy little spots I used to visit in my younger days. I especially loved the mushroom risotto – it was rich and hearty. The only minor hiccup was a slight delay in service during a busy lunch hour, but the charm of the place makes up for it. Highly recommend if you’re in the mood for a relaxed meal!
