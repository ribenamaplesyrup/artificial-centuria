**Place Name: Dalston Books & Brews**  
**Rating: ★★★★★**  
**Review:** Love this place! It’s the perfect spot to grab a cup of coffee and get lost in a good book. The staff is super friendly and they have an amazing collection of fantasy novels. Plus, they host book readings and events that make weekends really exciting. Definitely my go-to spot for winding down.

**Place Name: The Vegan Haven**  
**Rating: ★★★★☆**  
**Review:** I recently tried The Vegan Haven with a friend, and it was such a pleasant surprise! The menu is creative and the food is delicious. I'm not vegan, but I didn’t miss meat one bit. Only downside is that the service was a bit slow during peak hours. Other than that, it's a great place for a healthy meal.

**Place Name: Eastside Debate Academy**  
**Rating: ★★★★★**  
**Review:** As a member of the school debate team, I often attend workshops here, and they are fantastic. The coaches are knowledgeable and really encourage us to think critically. I've learned so much and made many friends. If you're into debate, this is the place to be!

**Place Name: Dalston Superstore**  
**Rating: ★★★★☆**  
**Review:** A fun spot with great music and good vibes. I love the quirky decor and the eclectic mix of people. It’s especially lively on weekends, though it can get crowded. Drinks are fairly priced, and the atmosphere is always welcoming.

**Place Name: Hackney Downs Park**  
**Rating: ★★★★★**  
**Review:** This park is such a gem! It's perfect for a morning jog or just hanging out with friends. I love coming here on sunny days to read or have a picnic. The playground is also quite nice for younger kids. Definitely one of the best green spaces in the area.

**Place Name: Emerald Nails**  
**Rating: ★★★★★**  
**Review:** I’ve been coming here with my mum for a while now, and we always leave happy. The staff is professional and really attentive to detail. The place is clean, and they have a wide range of nail colors to choose from. Highly recommend!

**Place Name: The National Gallery**  
**Rating: ★★★★★**  
**Review:** Visited during a school trip and was absolutely inspired by the art collection. There’s so much to see, from classic to modern art. It’s quite big, so plan your visit. The galleries are well laid out and entrance is free, which is amazing. Can’t wait to go again and maybe bring my family.

**Place Name: Pizza Union Dalston**  
**Rating: ★★★★☆**  
**Review:** Great spot for a quick and tasty meal. The pizzas are super affordable and come out really fast. Perfect for a student budget. It’s a bit loud, but that’s part of the fun vibe. Ideal for grabbing a bite with friends after school!
