**Weekly Grocery List**

**Fruits and Vegetables**  
- Bananas (6)  
- Apples (4)  
- Spinach (1 bag)  
- Carrots (500g)  
- Broccoli (1 head)  
- Tomatoes (4)  
- Onions (2)  
- Garlic (1 bulb)  
- Potatoes (1kg)  
- Bell peppers (2)

**Proteins**  
- Chicken breast (500g)  
- Eggs (dozen)  
- Canned chickpeas (2 cans)  
- Lean minced beef (500g)  

**Dairy**  
- Milk (1 litre)  
- Greek yogurt (500g)  
- Cheese (200g block)

**Grains and Pasta**  
- Whole grain bread (1 loaf)  
- Brown rice (1kg)  
- Whole wheat pasta (500g)

**Snacks**  
- Nuts (almonds or mixed nuts) (200g)  
- Dark chocolate (100g)  
- Popcorn (plain)  

**Condiments and Spices**  
- Olive oil (500ml)  
- Balsamic vinegar (250ml)  
- Salt and pepper  
- Dried herbs (mixed Italian herbs)  
- Soy sauce (small bottle)

**Frozen Items**  
- Frozen mixed vegetables (1 bag)  
- Frozen berries (for smoothies) (1 bag)

**Miscellaneous**  
- Coffee or tea  
- Breakfast cereal (oat-based)  
- Tinned tomatoes (2 cans)  

**Budget Consideration**  
- Focus on generic/store brands for cost-effective choices where available.
