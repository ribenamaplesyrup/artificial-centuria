Hannah Kowalczyk resides in a charming two-bedroom flat at Osier Grove Estate in Dalston, London, which she owns. Her home, located on the third floor of a vintage building, exudes character and warmth, reflecting her love for the arts. The decor embraces an eclectic mix of modern art pieces and antique furnishings collected over the years, showcasing her worldly taste and appreciation for creativity. Vibrant paintings, many of which are her own creations, adorn the walls, adding a personal touch to the space. A cozy reading nook by the window, filled with plants and natural light, offers a serene retreat for her frequent moments of reflection. Hannah's home is both tidy and inviting, with the scent of fresh flowers subtly enhancing the atmosphere, a testament to her keen eye for detail and lively spirit.
