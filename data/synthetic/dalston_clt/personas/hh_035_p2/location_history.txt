**Grace Taylor's Weekly Location Pattern**

**Home Location:**
- Primary Residence: 2 Baker's Lane Mews, Dalston, London

**Work/Commute Patterns:**
- Commutes to a creative studio located in Shoreditch, London, via cycling or public transport (bus or overground).
- Typical work hours: 9:30 AM to 6:30 PM, Monday to Friday.

**Regular Stops:**
- **Coffee Shop:** Often stops at "Brews & Blues" in Dalston for a morning coffee on her way to work, typically between 8:30 AM and 9:00 AM.
- **Lunch Breaks:** Frequently found at "The Plant Food Cafe" in Shoreditch from 1:00 PM to 2:00 PM, enjoying vegetarian dishes.
- **Gym:** Attends an evening yoga class at "Flow Yoga Studio" in Dalston every Tuesday and Thursday from 7:00 PM to 8:00 PM.
- **Gardening Supplies:** Visits "Green Fingers Garden Centre" in Hackney every other Saturday morning to purchase supplies for her balcony garden.

**Weekend Patterns:**
- **Saturday Mornings:** Starts with a visit to "Dalston Farmers' Market" between 9:00 AM and 11:00 AM for fresh produce.
- **Saturday Afternoons:** Enjoys exploring local art galleries such as "Dalston Gallery" or "Hackney Art House."
- **Saturday Evenings:** Often attends indie film screenings at "Arthouse Cinema" in Hackney, usually between 6:00 PM and 9:00 PM.
- **Sunday Mornings:** Relaxed brunch at "Garden Brunch Cafe" in Dalston, typically from 10:00 AM to 11:30 AM.
- **Sunday Afternoons:** Participates in community gardening projects or events in "Dalston Community Garden" from 1:00 PM to 4:00 PM.
- **Sunday Evenings:** Enjoys quiet evenings at home, often found reading on her balcony.

**Frequent Establishments:**
- "Brews & Blues" for daily coffee
- "The Plant Food Cafe" for lunch
- "Flow Yoga Studio" for yoga sessions
- Art galleries and community events in Dalston and Hackney
- "Arthouse Cinema" for film events

**Notes:**
Grace prefers walking or cycling when commuting within Dalston and makes conscious lifestyle choices aligning with her eco-friendly values.
